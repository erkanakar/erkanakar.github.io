Nonconformance and CAPA Management

Efficient, Proactive, Resilient

Navigate complexities with ease. Experience a proactive approach to identifying and resolving nonconformities.

Discover Our Unique Way

Nonconformance is All About Adopting a Proactive Quality Control Approach.

PQM equips you with the best tools to ensure standards and compliance.

Early Detection

PQM Mobile App helps controllers to detect deviations without miss by displaying target results and tolerance limits. Controllers can view parts specification documents with one click.

Timely Resolution

Implementing corrective actions swiftly ensures that production can continue with minimal disruption. Agile Corrective module is the perfect tool to track and manage the person or team tasks.

Immediate Containment

It's crucial to to implement measures to ensure that nonconformity does not propagate throughout the production process or reach the customer. PQM generates alerts to notify relevant users, integrates with your ERP to quarantine the defective part or inventory.

Thorough Documentation

Accurately recording the details of a nonconformity is crucial for traceability and analysis. PQM enables Quality Engineers to configure and revise NCR form templates and flows using a drag and drop interface.

Enhanced Responsiveness to Quality Issues

Agile Boards Meets the Quality

PQM's nonconformance and corrective action module revolutionizes the way production quality managers handle quality issues by introducing an agile approach to task management. PQM allows for an efficient workflow, adapting quickly to changes and priorities on the production floor

Dynamic
Task Boards

The agile board provides a visual and interactive platform for managing nonconformance and corrective actions. It allows managers to swiftly categorize tasks, adjust priorities, and monitor progress in real-time, ensuring a fluid response to quality issues as they arise.

Customizable
Workflow Stages

PQM allows for the creation of customizable stages within the workflow, tailored to the specific needs of the manufacturing environment. This flexibility ensures that the system aligns perfectly with the organization's unique processes and quality management practices.

Drag-and-Drop
Functionality

Enhancing user experience and efficiency, this feature enables managers to easily reorganize tasks based on changing priorities or status updates. This intuitive interface simplifies the process of updating task statuses from 'To Do' to 'In Progress' and finally to 'Completed'.

Real-Time Collaboration
and Updates

The agile board facilitates seamless collaboration among team members. It provides instant updates and notifications, ensuring all members are aligned and informed about task statuses, changes, or new corrective actions required.

Total Control Over Issues

Continous Oversight and In-Depth Analysis

Experience the detailed analytics and visualizations that transform your quality data into actionable intelligence,
enhancing decision-making and operational efficiency.

Instant Nonconformance Analytics

Dashboards and reports at location, production line/department, product, model, part, project, customer, or machine level.

Complete CAPA Visibility

Boost efficiency and productivity with PQM, featuring real-time operational dashboards for comprehensive analysis of adverse quality events.

Trend Analysis

Identify key trends in quality control using our Nonconformance Trend Analysis, which carefully monitors and assesses data at different quality checkpoints."
