Document Control

Simple, Smart, Compliant.

Finally, a document management solution tailored for manufacturers

Foolproof document control

Fast Track to digitize manufacturing quality plans

Smart Data Collection

Documents that Talk to Your Users

PQM document control is the smart way of digitizing your quality plans, manuals, work instructions and forms. Paving the way to a paperless world, it enables mobile data collection quick, easy and precise.

PQM document control simplifies revisioning, access control, compliance maintenance, and keeps you up-to-date with engineering, process and regulatory changes.

Confıgurable Platform

Implement Your Rules

Harness the power of adaptable workflows with the ease of customization. Configure steps, forms, fields for to align with industry best practices or tailor them uniquely, all without the need for coding.

PQM's digital document control software enables the creation of diverse process structures for a variaty of document types, such as quality plans, procedures, specifications, and work instructions. PQM allows you to setupt workflows and routing alternatives, offering limitless possibilities for managing your documents efficiently.

Automated Inspection
Planning & Scheduling

Shop Floor Inspection Planning has Never Been Easier.

  • Setup your own teams and configure task-to-team assignment via flexible rules.
  • Visualy track team utilization.
  • Easily arrange task generation rules to make sure QA teams will only receive the most relevant and timely inspection tasks.
  • Enrich your inspection task records for a better resource optimization.

Unique Needs, Unique Capabilities

Best Fit for Manufacturing Inspections

Dynamic Plan
Identifiers

Configurable quality plans allows the definition of your own identifier elements. PQM dynamically configures its dashboards to present what matters to you.

Supports Serial/Lot
Genealogy

PQM document control allows the visual break-down of inspection results, NCR's and Corrective Actions at all levels of your product lot/serial genealogy.

Mass Upload /
Update

Launching a whole new project or product next month? PQM enables csv upload of thousands of checkpoints and quality plans in a few minutes.

Designed For Fast Adoption, High Accuracy

Response Types

Mult-Language

Formulas
