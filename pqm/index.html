Completeness

PQM clears the fog in the shop floor. Inspectors are equipped with a clear task dashboard for all shop floor inspection needs. Incomplete data collection triggers alerts and prevents the sign-off for the manufactured part, mitigating the risks of missed quality checks.

Accuracy

PQM facilitates accurate data entry through guided questions and visual proof collection, drastically reducing human error. The modern mobile interface ensures entering results, logging defects, and assigning corrective actions are executed with precision.

Timeliness

With PQM, the data is captured into the mobile app right where it's observed, significantly speeding up the process and reducing the likelihood of errors. This immediate digital capture of data ensures that it is not only accurate but also instantly available for analysis and action.
