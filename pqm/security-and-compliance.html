  • Security & Compliance

Security & Trust at
PQM's Core

At the PQM Trust Center, we're dedicated to safeguarding your data with the most rigorous security measures and ensuring our compliance practices exceed industry standards

Data Privacy at Its Core

Unwavering Commitment to GDPR Compliance

At PQM, we understand the importance of data privacy and protection in today’s digital world. Our platform is designed with GDPR compliance at its core, ensuring that all customer data is handled with the utmost care and security. We employ stringent data processing agreements, uphold individuals' rights to data privacy, and implement comprehensive data protection measures.

User & Device Authentication

Robust User & Device Authentication Mechanisms

PQM prioritizes security from the ground up, starting with rigorous user and device authentication to ensure that only authorized personnel can access the system. Our multifactor authentication (MFA) process requires more than one method of verification from independent categories of credentials, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access.

Authorization & Access Controls

Fine-Grained Authorization and Access Controls

PQM’s platform is built on a foundation of robust authorization and access control mechanisms, enabling precise management over who can see and do what within the system. Our role-based access control (RBAC) model allow for detailed and flexible permission settings, ensuring that users only access the information and functionalities pertinent to their roles.

API & Document Encryption

Comprehensive API &
Document Encryption

We employ state-of-the-art encryption technologies to protect all data in transit and at rest within our platform. With advanced SSL encryption for network security and AES-256 bit encryption for documents and data storage, PQM ensures that your sensitive information is always encrypted and secure from unauthorized access. Our commitment to encryption excellence means you can trust that your data is protected by the highest standards of security.
