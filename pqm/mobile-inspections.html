Mobile Inspections

Fast, Accurate, Easy

Game-changing mobile experience and real time shop floor collaboration.

PQM is the Companion for a Successful Paperless Initiative

Everything You Need to Eliminate Paper in the Shop Floor

Available for Mobile and Web

Adaptive & Intuitive User Interface

PQM's mobile and web interface simplifies the inspection process, making it accessible to users of all skill levels.

  • Clean and responsive design for both phones and tablets.
  • 2 different design options: "Compact" for high volume data collection, "Comfortable" for slow-pace inspections.
  • Minimum keyboard usage needed.
  • Color coded status indicators and intuitive icons.
  • Guides and hints available with one-click.

Available for Mobile and Web

Photo Capturing, Tagging and Reporting

Capture clear, detailed photos during inspections to provide visual evidence of findings. This feature allows for a more comprehensive understanding of inspection areas and issues.

  • Capture or upload images directly using mobile device camera and attach to inspections, nonconformances or corrective actions.
  • Add annonations, auto-generate text on image for better clarity and tracking.

Real-Time Collaboration in the Shop-Floor

Shop Floor Inspection Planning has Never Been Easier.

Capture and manage non-conformances as they occur. The PQM platform ensures that team members are always aligned, facilitating swift decision-making and enhancing overall efficiency in inspections and nonconformance processes.

  • Enter nonconformances using mobile app by associating them with a specific checkpoint, out of spec finding or inspection task representing a manufactured part or process.
  • Notify relevant teams of their nonconformance or corrective action tasks instantly.

Team members can exchange information by entering comments on nonconformance forms.

Enchance Process Compliance

Full Visibility Over Your Inspection Tasks

Increase accountability and task ownership, better plan and utilizate your resources, adhere to regulatory standards and internal quality benchmarks.

  • PQM allows automated tracking of each step in the data collection and nonconformance handling processes.

Mobile reminders, task dashboards at team, manager and location levels help expedite task completions and mitigations.
